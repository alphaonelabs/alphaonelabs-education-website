What is a Chapter?

Alpha One Chapters are local, community-led groups that gather around shared values of education, curiosity, and collaboration. Chapters host events, meetups, workshops, and co-learning sessions.

What we're looking for:

  • Passionate educators and community organizers
  • Commitment to inclusive, collaborative learning
  • Ability to host regular events or meetups
  • Alignment with Alpha One Labs values
