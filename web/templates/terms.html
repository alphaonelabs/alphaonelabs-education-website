Terms of Service

1. Commission Structure

Alpha One Labs operates on a revenue-sharing model where:

  • Teachers receive 90% of the course and session fees
  • Alpha One Labs retains a 10% commission to cover platform maintenance, payment processing, and support services
  • Payments are processed automatically through Stripe and distributed to teachers' connected accounts

2. Teacher Payments

To receive payments, teachers must:

  • Create and connect a Stripe account
  • Provide necessary tax information and banking details
  • Maintain compliance with Stripe's terms of service
  • Keep their payment information up to date

3. Platform Rules

All users must adhere to the following rules:

  • Provide accurate and truthful information
  • Respect intellectual property rights
  • Maintain professional conduct
  • Follow content guidelines

4. Course Content

Teachers are responsible for:

  • Creating and maintaining high-quality course content
  • Ensuring content accuracy and relevance
  • Responding to student inquiries in a timely manner
  • Updating course materials as needed

5. Refund Policy

Our refund policy includes:

  • 30-day money-back guarantee for course purchases
  • Pro-rated refunds for subscription-based courses
  • No refunds for completed individual sessions
  • Case-by-case evaluation for special circumstances

6. Privacy and Data Protection

We are committed to:

  • Protecting user data and privacy
  • Complying with relevant data protection regulations
  • Securing payment information
  • Maintaining transparent data practices

7. Platform Changes

Alpha One Labs reserves the right to:

  • Modify these terms with notice to users
  • Update platform features and functionality
  • Adjust commission rates with advance notice
  • Change payment processing methods if necessary
